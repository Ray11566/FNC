About FNATIC ORG

Professional BGMI Organization

FNATIC ORG is a world-renowned BGMI esports organization founded on October 1, 2025, dedicated exclusively to Battlegrounds Mobile India.

Our mission is to push the boundaries of competitive mobile gaming, foster talent, and create unforgettable experiences for our global fanbase.

With state-of-the-art training facilities and a dedicated coaching staff, we provide our players with the resources they need to perform at their peak in BGMI tournaments.
